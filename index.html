Our Story

Grooming with Excellence

The Moody Fade Legacy

Located in the heart of East Vancouver at 2255 E Hastings Street, Moody Fade Barbershop has built a reputation for precision cuts, expert fades, and a welcoming atmosphere that keeps clients coming back.

Our skilled team brings years of experience to every service. We believe looking sharp isn't a luxury — it's a standard every man deserves.

Our Philosophy

We combine traditional barbering techniques with modern precision. Every cut is tailored to the individual — your face shape, your style, your personality.

From a quick buzz cut to a full deluxe package, we approach every service with the same care and attention to detail.
